1. Home.

Hank Green's website is represented as a reference. His page has a full-screen background image in the header and navigation at the top that remains at the top even when scrolled down. It includes sections for his novels, about him, and a footer.
Here is the link for his page.https://hankgreen.com/

On this website, there is also a header with a full-screen backdrop image and a brief introduction. The navigation bar appears at the top of the page as you navigate down the page. Then there's a section for assignments, which contains an illustration and a concise description of the assignments. There is also an education section that includes the names of the schools and colleges from where the creator had completed her schooling and +2 Level. At last, it has a footer section with the same navigation bar as before, except that it is not fixed, and the footer then has social media icons with links to the relevant pages.

On Hank Green's website, the navigation bar is at the top of the page, while it is below the header on this page. Furthermore, the book section contains only an image and a short description, while the assignment on this page contains an image that links to the assignment's pdf.
